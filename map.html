Lhasa · Snowy Region Bangdian Workshop

Nestled in the century-old alleys of Barkhor Street in Lhasa, it boasts a 120-year history of Bangdian making. It inherits the most authentic traditional Tibetan weaving techniques, adheres to natural dyes and hand weaving, serves as a core inheritance base of Bangdian intangible cultural heritage, and is a key traditional workshop partnered with Hongzhi Translation Bridge.

Cultural Decoding Class Natural Dyeing Craft Face-to-face with ICH Craftsmen Traditional Weaving Experience

Address: No. 108, Barkhor Street, Chengguan District, Lhasa

Opening Hours: 9:00 - 18:00 (Open all year round, bilingual tour guide)

Xigaze · Plateau Skilled Handicraft Workshop

Located in Samzhubzê District, Xigaze, it is a key innovative workshop partnered with Hongzhi Translation Bridge. It focuses on integrating traditional Bangdian patterns with modern aesthetics, specializing in digital Bangdian design and cross-cultural aesthetic creation, supporting online design to physical production, and is deeply popular among young people and international tourists.

Bangdian Innovative Design Digital Bangdian Production Immersive Creation Cross-cultural Aesthetic Salon

Address: No. 56, Everest Road, Samzhubzê District, Xigaze

Opening Hours: 9:30 - 19:00 (Open all year round, cross-border reservation available)
